## 1. Overview

This document provides the complete Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) tax filing schedule for 2025-2026, including filing deadlines, approval workflow, and process requirements.

**Purpose:**
- Ensure 100% on-time BIR tax compliance
- Define clear approval workflow and timelines
- Provide reference for all statutory filing deadlines
- Document required lead times for preparation and approval

**Scope:**
- All monthly, quarterly, and annual BIR returns
- All tax payment requirements
- All approval and filing procedures

## 2. Tax Filing Process Overview

### 2.1 Four-Step Tax Filing Process

```
┌────────────────┐     ┌────────────────┐     ┌────────────────┐     ┌────────────────┐
│   STEP 1:      │     │   STEP 2:      │     │   STEP 3:      │     │   STEP 4:      │
│  PREPARATION   │────▶│ REPORT APPROVAL│────▶│PAYMENT APPROVAL│────▶│ FILING & PAY   │
│                │     │                │     │                │     │                │
│ BIR Deadline   │     │ BIR Deadline   │     │ BIR Deadline   │     │ BIR Deadline   │
│    - 4 days    │     │    - 2 days    │     │    - 1 day     │     │   (On Date)    │
└────────────────┘     └────────────────┘     └────────────────┘     └────────────────┘
   Finance               Senior Finance         Finance              Finance
   Supervisor            Manager                Director             Supervisor
   (JPL/CNVC)           (CNVC/RM)              (GVKC)              (JPL)
```

### 2.2 Step-by-Step Process Detail

| Step | Process Detail | Responsible | Target Deadline | Deliverables |
|------|----------------|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| **1. Preparation** | Gather data, prepare BIR form, draft payment request (check voucher) | Finance Supervisor (JPL) | **BIR Deadline - 4 business days** | BIR form (draft), supporting schedules, payment voucher (draft) |
| **2. Report Approval** | Senior Finance Manager reviews and approves report/data for accuracy and compliance. File request submitted to FD. | Senior Finance Manager (CNVC/RM) | **BIR Deadline - 2 business days** | Approved BIR form, file request memo |
| **3. Payment Approval** | Finance Director reviews and approves release of funds for remittance | Finance Director (GVKC) | **BIR Deadline - 1 business day** | Approved payment voucher, authorized payment |
| **4. Filing & Payment** | File return electronically via eFPS or over-the-counter. Remit payment to authorized agent bank. | Finance Supervisor (JPL) | **On or before BIR Deadline** | eFPS confirmation, bank payment receipt |

## 7. Penalties for Late Filing

### 7.1 Surcharges and Interest

| Violation | Surcharge | Interest | Compromise Penalty |
|-----------|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| **Late Filing** | 25% of tax due | 12% per annum from due date until paid | Varies per RR |
| **Late Payment** | 25% of tax due | 12% per annum from due date until paid | Varies per RR |
| **Non-Filing** | 25% of tax due + 50% of tax due (fraudulent) | 20% per annum from due date until paid | Double the tax due |
| **Incorrect Return** | 25% of deficiency tax | 12% per annum from due date until paid | Varies |

**Example Calculation:**
- Tax Due: PHP 50,000
- Due Date: January 10, 2026
- Filed: January 15, 2026 (5 days late)
- **Surcharge:** PHP 50,000 × 25% = PHP 12,500
- **Interest:** PHP 50,000 × 12% × (5/365) = PHP 82.19
- **Total Amount Due:** PHP 50,000 + PHP 12,500 + PHP 82.19 = **PHP 62,582.19**

### 7.2 Administrative Penalties

- **Failure to File:** PHP 1,000 to PHP 25,000 fine and/or imprisonment
- **Failure to Issue Receipts/Invoices:** PHP 5,000 to PHP 25,000 fine and/or imprisonment
- **Failure to Register:** PHP 5,000 to PHP 25,000 fine and/or imprisonment

## 8. Tax Filing Controls and Procedures

### 8.1 Pre-Filing Checklist

**Before filing any BIR return, verify:**

☐ All source data is complete and accurate
☐ Form is properly filled out (no blank required fields)
☐ Computations have been verified and reconciled to GL
☐ Supporting schedules are attached
☐ Return has been reviewed by Senior Finance Manager
☐ Return has been approved by Finance Director
☐ Payment voucher has been prepared and approved
☐ Taxpayer information is correct (TIN, name, address)
☐ Filing deadline is confirmed (accounting for holidays/weekends)

### 8.2 Filing Methods

**Primary Method: BIR eFPS (Electronic Filing and Payment System)**
- URL: https://efps.bir.gov.ph
- Requires eFPS enrollment and user ID/password
- Available 24/7 (except during system maintenance)
- Generates transaction reference number upon successful filing

**Backup Method: Over-the-Counter Filing**
- File at Revenue District Office (RDO) where registered
- Submit original and copy of return
- Obtain stamped acknowledgment copy
- Use only if eFPS is unavailable or technical issues

### 8.3 Payment Methods

**Preferred: Electronic Payment**
- Through BIR eFPS portal
- Through authorized agent bank's online platform
- Generates electronic payment confirmation

**Alternative: Over-the-Counter Payment**
- Pay at authorized agent bank (see BIR website for list)
- Present tax return form and payment voucher
- Obtain stamped bank validation and official receipt

### 8.4 Post-Filing Procedures

After filing each return:

1. **Save confirmation:**
   - eFPS transaction reference number
   - Bank payment receipt
   - Email confirmation (if applicable)

2. **Archive documents:**
   - Filed tax return (PDF)
   - Supporting schedules
   - Payment confirmation
   - File in: `Close_[YYYY-MM]/03_Tax_Returns/[Form]/`

3. **Update tax filing log:**
   - Form filed
   - Period covered
   - Filing date
   - Transaction reference number
   - Amount paid

4. **Distribute copies:**
   - Finance Director (notification email)
   - CFO (monthly summary)
   - Tax advisor (for annual reconciliation)

## 11. Tax Audit and BIR Examination Preparedness

### 11.1 Required Records for BIR Audit

Keep readily available (in case of BIR audit):

1. **Filed tax returns** (all periods within statute of limitations - 3 years or 10 years if fraud)
2. **Proof of payment** (bank validations, official receipts)
3. **Supporting schedules** (sales/purchases registers, withholding tax schedules)
4. **Books of accounts** (general ledger, journals)
5. **Financial statements**
6. **Invoices and receipts** (both sales and purchases)
7. **Contracts and agreements**
8. **BIR registration documents** (Certificate of Registration, TIN card)
9. **Authority to Print** (receipts/invoices)

### 11.2 BIR Audit Response Protocol

If BIR Letter of Authority (LOA) is received:

1. **Immediate notification:** Finance Director + CFO within 24 hours
2. **Assemble documents:** Per BIR request within prescribed timeline
3. **Coordinate with tax advisor:** Engage external tax counsel
4. **Designate point person:** Typically Finance Director or Tax Manager
5. **Prepare conference room:** Provide workspace for BIR examiners
6. **Document all interactions:** Minutes of meetings, document requests, submissions
7. **Escalate issues:** To CFO/CEO for significant findings or assessments
