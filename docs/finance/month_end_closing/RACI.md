## RACI Legend

| Code | Role | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| **R** | **Responsible** | Person who performs the work to complete the task |
| **A** | **Accountable** | Person ultimately answerable for correct and thorough completion (only ONE per task) |
| **C** | **Consulted** | People who provide input, subject matter expertise, or must be consulted |
| **I** | **Informed** | People who must be kept informed of progress or decisions |

## Key Principles for RACI Effectiveness

1. **One Accountable per Task:** Only one person can be Accountable (the "A"). This ensures clear ownership.

2. **Responsible Does the Work:** The "R" is the doer. Multiple people can be Responsible if they share the work.

3. **Consult Before Deciding:** "C" roles provide input before the task is completed or decision is made.

4. **Inform After Deciding:** "I" roles are notified after the task is completed or decision is made.

5. **Avoid RACI Bloat:** Not every role needs to be involved in every task. Only assign RACI roles where genuinely needed.

6. **Clear Escalation:** When the Responsible person cannot complete the task, they escalate to the Accountable person.
