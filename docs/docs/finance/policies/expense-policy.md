# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Policy Statement

This policy establishes guidelines for business expense reimbursements to ensure compliance with tax regulations, financial controls, and ethical business practices. All employees must adhere to these guidelines when incurring business expenses.

**Policy Number**: FIN-002
**Effective Date**: January 1, 2025
**Last Reviewed**: January 15, 2025
**Policy Owner**: Chief Financial Officer
**Approval Authority**: CEO & Board of Directors

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees (full-time, part-time, contract)
- Board members and advisors
- Authorized consultants and contractors
- All business-related expenses

---

## General Principles

### 1. Business Purpose Requirement
All expenses must have a legitimate business purpose and be necessary for conducting company business. Personal expenses are strictly prohibited.

### 2. Reasonableness Standard
Expenses must be reasonable in amount and appropriate for the business context. Excessive or extravagant expenses will not be reimbursed.

### 3. Prior Approval
Certain expenses require pre-approval. Failure to obtain approval may result in non-reimbursement.

### 4. Timely Submission
Expense reports must be submitted within **60 days** of incurring the expense. Late submissions may be denied.

### 5. Documentation
Original receipts required for all expenses exceeding ₱500. Digital receipts acceptable if legible and complete.

## Eligible Expenses

### Transportation

| Expense Type | Policy | Limit | Approval Required |
|-------------|--------|-------|------------------|
| **Local taxi/ride-share** | Reimbursable for business meetings | ₱500 per trip | Manager |
| **Mileage (personal vehicle)** | ₱15/km | Based on actual distance | Manager |
| **Parking fees** | Reimbursable with receipt | Actual cost | Manager |
| **Public transportation** | Reimbursable (MRT, bus, jeep) | Actual cost | Manager |
| **Domestic flights** | Economy class only | Market rate | Manager |
| **International flights** | Economy class | Market rate | CFO (>$1,000) |

**Note**: Commuting between home and regular office location is not reimbursable.

---

### Meals and Entertainment

| Expense Type | Per Person Limit | Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Business meals (solo)** | ₱500 | Rarely approved; justification required |
| **Client entertainment** | ₱2,000 | Names of attendees required |
| **Team meals** | ₱800 | Attendee list required |
| **Alcohol** | ₱300 per person | Pre-approval required |
| **Per diem (travel)** | ₱1,500/day | Domestic travel >100km from office |

**Requirements for Entertainment Expenses**:
1. List all attendees (name, company, title)
2. State business purpose
3. Itemized receipt required
4. Pre-approval for >₱10,000

---

### Accommodation

| Location Type | Per Night Limit | Notes |
|--------------|----------------|-------|
| **Metro Manila** | ₱5,000 | Mid-tier business hotel |
| **Provincial cities** | ₱3,500 | Reasonable accommodation |
| **International - Asia** | $150 USD | City-dependent |
| **International - US/Europe** | $200 USD | City-dependent |

**Booking Requirements**:
- Use corporate travel partner when available
- Book economy/standard rooms
- Early booking encouraged for best rates
- Upgrades at personal expense

---

### Conference and Training

| Expense Type | Policy | Approval |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Registration fees** | Reimbursable if business-related | Manager (< ₱20K), CFO (> ₱20K) |
| **Materials/books** | Reimbursable if job-related | Manager |
| **Certification fees** | Reimbursable if job-required | Department Head |

**Pre-Approval Required**: All conferences/training >₱50,000

---

### Office Supplies and Equipment

| Expense Type | Limit | Policy |
|-------------|-------|--------|
| **Office supplies** | ₱2,000 | Prefer procurement process |
| **Software/subscriptions** | ₱5,000 | IT approval required |
| **Equipment** | ₱10,000 | Use purchase request process |

**Note**: Recurring needs should go through standard procurement, not expense reimbursement.

---

## Ineligible Expenses

The following are **NOT** reimbursable under any circumstances:

❌ Personal entertainment or recreation
❌ Commuting to/from regular office
❌ Personal grooming (haircuts, spa, etc.)
❌ Gym memberships or fitness classes
❌ Personal clothing or accessories
❌ Fines, penalties, traffic violations
❌ Political contributions
❌ Personal legal fees
❌ Lost personal items
❌ Late payment fees or interest charges
❌ Pet care or personal errands

## Submission Requirements

### Required Information
1. Expense date and description
2. Business purpose and participants
3. Amount in original currency
4. Payment method (personal card, cash, corporate card)
5. Project/client code (if applicable)
6. Digital receipt image (for >₱500)

### Receipt Requirements

| Amount | Receipt Required | Alternative Accepted |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Up to ₱200 | No | Employee declaration |
| ₱201 - ₱500 | No | Strongly recommended |
| Over ₱500 | **Yes (mandatory)** | Affidavit if lost (max 3/year) |

**Receipt Quality Standards**:
- Legible vendor name and date
- Clear itemization
- Total amount visible
- VAT details (if applicable)

---

## Special Situations

### Lost Receipt
**Allowance**: 3 per calendar year
**Process**:
1. Submit "Missing Receipt Affidavit"
2. Provide alternative proof (credit card statement, email confirmation)
3. Requires manager justification
4. Finance Controller approval for >₱2,000

---

### International Travel
**Additional Requirements**:
- Pre-trip approval recommended
- Currency conversion using Philippine Central Bank (BSP) rate on transaction date
- Immigration stamps or boarding passes may be requested
- Daily expense diary for trips >5 days

---

### Client Gifts
**Policy**: Maximum ₱5,000 per gift
**Requirements**:
- Pre-approval required
- Business justification
- Recipient name and company
- Cultural/holiday appropriateness
- Compliance with anti-corruption laws

---

### Urgent/Emergency Expenses
**Protocol**:
1. Verbal approval from manager (documented)
2. Submit expense report within 48 hours
3. Provide incident report
4. Post-approval review by Finance

## Corporate Credit Card Program

### Eligibility
- Directors and above (automatic)
- Manager-level (with approval)
- Frequent travelers (upon request)

### Responsibilities
- Submit expense reports within 10 days of statement
- Maintain records and receipts
- Personal charges prohibited
- Report lost/stolen cards immediately

### Violations
- Personal use: Immediate repayment required + warning
- Repeated violations: Card cancellation + disciplinary action

---

## Tax and Compliance

### Philippines BIR Requirements
- Proper expense documentation for tax deductibility
- Entertainment expenses subject to special rules
- Withholding tax may apply to certain expenses
- 7-year retention of all expense records

### Anti-Corruption Compliance
All expenses must comply with:
- Philippines Anti-Graft and Corrupt Practices Act
- US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (if applicable)
- UK Bribery Act (if applicable)

**Prohibited**: Payments to government officials without legal basis and prior approval

---

## Non-Compliance and Penalties

### First Violation
- Warning letter
- Mandatory policy training
- Additional scrutiny for 6 months

### Second Violation
- Formal written warning
- Manager notification
- Suspension of reimbursement privileges (30 days)

### Third Violation
- Disciplinary action up to termination
- Repayment of improperly claimed expenses
- Potential legal action if fraud suspected

---

## Audit and Monitoring

**Random Audits**: 10% of expense reports selected quarterly
**Targeted Audits**: High-value claims, unusual patterns
**Annual Review**: Policy effectiveness and compliance rates

**Audit Rights**: Finance reserves the right to request additional documentation at any time.

---

## Policy Exceptions

**Exception Request Process**:
1. Submit written request to Finance Controller
2. Detailed justification required
3. Supporting documentation
4. CEO approval required for exceptions >₱100,000

**Common Exception Scenarios**:
- Exceptional business circumstances
- Client relationship requirements
- Industry norms
- Emergency situations
