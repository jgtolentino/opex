# RACI Matrix - Month-End Closing

**Purpose**: Clear accountability and responsibility assignment for all month-end close tasks

**RACI Legend**:
- **R** = Responsible (Does the work)
- **A** = Accountable (Final decision maker, sign-off authority)
- **C** = Consulted (Provides input, expertise)
- **I** = Informed (Kept updated on progress)

---

## Roles Defined

### Finance SSC Manager (Finance Director - FD)
- Strategic oversight of Finance Shared Services Center
- Final approval of financial statements
- Policy compliance and automation sponsor
- Budget owner and resource allocation

### Senior Finance Manager (SFM)
- Review and approval of all reconciliations
- Review and approval of all journal entries
- BIR filing oversight and approval
- Financial statement review before final approval
- Period lock authority

### Finance Supervisor (FS)
- Day-to-day execution of month-end close tasks
- Preparation of journal entries and reconciliations
- BIR form preparation and filing
- Financial statement preparation
- Coordination with Accounting Staff

### Accounting Staff (AS)
- Data entry and transaction processing
- Report generation from ERP
- Document preparation and archiving
- Cash advance processing
- Reconciliation support

## Escalation Matrix

### Issue Severity Levels

**Level 1: Minor** (Does not impact close timeline)
- Escalate to: Finance Supervisor
- Response time: 4 hours
- Resolution time: Same day

**Level 2: Moderate** (May impact close timeline)
- Escalate to: Senior Finance Manager
- Response time: 2 hours
- Resolution time: Within 1 business day

**Level 3: Major** (Will impact close timeline or BIR deadline)
- Escalate to: Finance SSC Manager
- Response time: 1 hour
- Resolution time: Immediate action required

**Level 4: Critical** (Prevents close completion or BIR filing)
- Escalate to: Finance SSC Manager + External Resources (IT, Legal, Auditors)
- Response time: Immediate
- Resolution time: All-hands-on-deck until resolved

## Communication Protocols

### Daily Standup (Day 1-14)
- **Time**: 9:00 AM daily
- **Attendees**: Finance Supervisor, Accounting Staff
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Format**: Progress update, blockers, next steps

### Review Meetings
| Meeting | Day | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----|-----------|---------|
| Bank Reconciliation Review | Day 2 | FS, SFM | Approve bank recons |
| Journal Entry Review | Day 10 | FS, SFM | Approve all JEs |
| Financial Statement Review | Day 13 | FS, SFM, FD | Review financial statements |
| Final Approval | Day 14 | FS, SFM, FD | Sign-off on financials |

### Status Reporting
- **Daily**: Finance Supervisor → Senior Finance Manager (email summary)
- **Weekly**: Senior Finance Manager → Finance SSC Manager (dashboard update)
- **Monthly**: Finance SSC Manager → Executive Team (close completion report)
