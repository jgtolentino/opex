## Filing Process Workflow

### Step 1: Preparation
- **Responsible**: Finance Supervisor
- **Target Completion**: BIR Deadline – 4 Business Days before
- **Activities**:
  - Gather data from ERP/payroll systems
  - Prepare the BIR Form
  - Draft payment request (check vouchers)
  - Complete all required schedules and attachments

### Step 2: Report Approval
- **Responsible**: Senior Finance Manager
- **Target Completion**: BIR Deadline – 2 Business Days before
- **Activities**:
  - Review report for accuracy and compliance
  - Verify calculations and supporting documentation
  - Approve the form for filing
  - Submit file request to Finance Director

### Step 3: Payment Approval
- **Responsible**: Finance Director
- **Target Completion**: BIR Deadline – 1 Business Day before
- **Activities**:
  - Review approved report and payment request
  - Approve the release of funds for remittance
  - Authorize payment processing

### Step 4: Filing & Payment
- **Responsible**: Finance Supervisor
- **Target Completion**: On or before the BIR Deadline
- **Activities**:
  - File the approved form electronically via eFPS
  - Remit payment via authorized agent bank or online
  - Archive confirmation receipts and transaction reference numbers
  - Update filing log and notify stakeholders

## Filing Methods

### Electronic Filing (eFPS)

**Required for**:
- All VAT-registered taxpayers
- Large taxpayers (as classified by BIR)
- Taxpayers with ₱50M+ annual income tax

**System**: BIR Electronic Filing and Payment System (eFPS)

**Advantages**:
- 24/7 availability
- Instant confirmation receipt
- Reduced processing time
- Electronic payment integration

### Manual Filing

**Available for**:
- Small taxpayers (case-by-case basis)
- System downtime emergencies

**Process**:
- Submit to Revenue District Office (RDO)
- Payment via authorized agent bank
- Physical confirmation stamped copy required

## Compliance Notes

### Critical Requirements
1. **Timely Filing**: Late filing incurs 25% surcharge on tax due + 20% interest per annum
2. **Accurate Data**: Ensure all withholding certificates match BIR forms
3. **Complete Documentation**: Maintain all supporting schedules for 10 years
4. **Electronic Signatures**: Ensure authorized signatory digital certificates are valid

### BIR Form Versions
- **Form 1601-C**: January 2018 version (with DPA)
- **Form 2550Q**: April 2024 version (ENCS)
- **Form 1702-Q**: Check BIR website for latest version

### Weekend/Holiday Adjustments
- If deadline falls on weekend or holiday, filing deadline moves to next business day
- Always check BIR website for official holiday announcements
