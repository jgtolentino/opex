# Finance Policies & Standard Operating Procedures

**Purpose**: Comprehensive policies and SOPs governing Finance SSC operations

**Scope**: All finance operations across RIM, CKVC, BOM, JPAL, JLI, JAP, LAS, RMQB agencies

**Compliance Framework**: Philippine GAAP, BIR regulations, PFRS standards

---

## Core Financial Policies

### 1. Month-End Close Policy

**Category**: Financial Reporting
**Primary Owner**: Finance SSC Manager
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly
**Link**: [README.md](./README.md)

**Policy Statement**:
All agencies must complete month-end financial close within 12 business days following month-end, adhering to standardized procedures and timelines.

**Key Requirements**:
- Bank reconciliation complete by Day 5
- All journal entries posted by Day 10
- Financial statements ready for review by Day 12
- Period locked by Day 15
- 100% documentation archived within close period

**Approvals Required**:
- Finance Supervisor: Daily task execution
- Senior Finance Manager: Review and approval of reconciliations and JEs
- Finance SSC Manager: Final sign-off on financial statements

---

### 2. BIR Tax Filing Policy

**Category**: Tax Compliance
**Primary Owner**: Senior Finance Manager
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly (or upon BIR regulation changes)
**Link**: [bir_schedule.md](./bir_schedule.md)

**Policy Statement**:
All BIR tax forms must be filed on or before statutory deadlines with complete supporting documentation and payment remittance.

**Key Requirements**:
- Form 1601-C filed by 10th day of following month
- Form 2550Q filed by 25th day following quarter-end
- Electronic filing via eFPS required for all forms
- 100% zero late filing compliance target
- Complete supporting schedules maintained for 10 years

**Workflow**:
1. Preparation (BIR Deadline – 4 days)
2. Report Approval (BIR Deadline – 2 days)
3. Payment Approval (BIR Deadline – 1 day)
4. Filing & Payment (On or before deadline)

---

### 3. Bank Reconciliation Policy

**Category**: Cash Management
**Primary Owner**: Finance Supervisor
**Review Frequency**: Monthly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-1-2-bank-reconciliation)

**Policy Statement**:
All bank accounts must be reconciled monthly with 100% accuracy within 2 business days of statement availability.

**Key Requirements**:
- Download official bank statements by Day 1
- Import and match transactions by Day 2
- Investigate all unmatched items same day
- Senior Finance Manager approval required before Day 5
- Maximum 5% unmatched items tolerance (requires escalation)

**Reconciliation Scope**:
- All operating accounts
- All savings/investment accounts
- All foreign currency accounts
- All credit card accounts

---

### 4. Accounts Payable & Receivable Policy

**Category**: Working Capital Management
**Primary Owner**: Finance Supervisor
**Review Frequency**: Monthly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-3-4-apAR-reconciliation)

**Policy Statement**:
AP and AR sub-ledgers must be reconciled to general ledger with aging analysis completed within 4 business days of month-end.

**Key Requirements**:
- AP aging report generated by Day 3
- AR aging report generated by Day 4
- 100% sub-ledger to GL reconciliation
- Bad debt provision review for AR &gt;90 days
- Payment scheduling for overdue AP

**Aging Categories**:
- Current (0-30 days)
- 31-60 days
- 61-90 days
- 91-120 days
- Over 120 days

### 5. Fixed Assets & Depreciation Policy

**Category**: Asset Management
**Primary Owner**: Finance Supervisor
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-5-6-fixed-assets--depreciation)

**Policy Statement**:
All fixed assets must be recorded in the asset register with depreciation calculated using straight-line method per BIR guidelines.

**Key Requirements**:
- Asset register updated for all purchases/disposals by Day 5
- Depreciation calculated monthly using straight-line method
- Capitalization threshold: ₱10,000
- Useful life per BIR Schedule: Buildings (20Y), Equipment (5Y), Furniture (10Y), Vehicles (5Y)
- Annual physical asset verification

**Depreciation Formula**:
```
Monthly Depreciation = (Cost - Salvage Value) / (Useful Life in Months)
```

### 6. Journal Entry Policy

**Category**: General Accounting
**Primary Owner**: Finance Supervisor
**Review Frequency**: Monthly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-9-10-journal-entries--accruals)

**Policy Statement**:
All journal entries must be properly supported, reviewed, and approved before posting to general ledger.

**Key Requirements**:
- All JEs require supporting documentation
- Senior Finance Manager approval required for all month-end JEs
- Finance SSC Manager approval required for JEs >₱100,000
- Recurring JEs documented in standing schedule
- All accruals must reverse in following period
- Maximum 2-day lag between JE preparation and posting

**JE Types**:
- Recurring (monthly standard entries)
- Accruals (expense recognition)
- Prepayment amortization
- Reclassifications
- Adjusting entries
- Reversing entries

### 7. Financial Statement Policy

**Category**: Financial Reporting
**Primary Owner**: Senior Finance Manager
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-11-12-financial-statements)

**Policy Statement**:
Financial statements must be prepared in accordance with Philippine GAAP and PFRS standards, with comparative prior period data and comprehensive notes.

**Key Requirements**:
- Income Statement prepared by Day 11
- Balance Sheet prepared by Day 11
- Cash Flow Statement prepared by Day 12
- Notes to financial statements completed by Day 12
- Variance analysis vs budget included
- Senior Finance Manager review by Day 13
- Finance SSC Manager approval by Day 14

**Statement Components**:
1. Income Statement (P&L)
2. Balance Sheet
3. Statement of Cash Flows (indirect method)
4. Statement of Changes in Equity
5. Notes to Financial Statements

---

### 8. Period Lock Policy

**Category**: System Controls
**Primary Owner**: Senior Finance Manager
**Review Frequency**: Monthly
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-15-period-lock--archive)

**Policy Statement**:
Accounting periods must be locked after month-end close completion to prevent unauthorized posting.

**Key Requirements**:
- Period locked by Day 15 following all approvals
- Only Senior Finance Manager can unlock periods
- Period unlock requires written justification and Finance SSC Manager approval
- All post-close adjustments documented in adjustment log
- Maximum 2 post-close adjustments per closed period

---

### 9. Document Retention Policy

**Category**: Compliance & Audit
**Primary Owner**: Finance SSC Manager
**Review Frequency**: Annually
**Link**: [tasks.md](./tasks.md#day-15-period-lock--archive)

**Policy Statement**:
All financial documents must be retained for minimum 10 years per BIR requirements with organized digital and physical archiving.

**Retention Schedule**:
- **10 years**: BIR forms, tax returns, audit reports, financial statements
- **7 years**: General ledger, journals, invoices, receipts
- **5 years**: Bank statements, reconciliations, payroll records
- **Permanent**: Corporate documents, contracts, legal agreements

**Storage Requirements**:
- Digital: Shared drive with access controls (primary)
- Physical: Locked filing cabinets (backup)
- Cloud backup: Weekly automated backup to Supabase Storage

---

**Category**: IT Controls
**Primary Owner**: Finance SSC Manager
**Review Frequency**: Quarterly
