# Month-End Closing Framework

**Purpose**: Standardized month-end close process for multi-agency Finance Shared Services Center

**Agencies Covered**: RIM, CKVC, BOM, JPAL, JLI, JAP, LAS, RMQB

**Closing Timeline**: Day 1-15 of following month

---

## Overview

This framework provides a structured approach to month-end financial closing across all agencies, ensuring:
- **Consistency**: Standardized processes across all agencies
- **Compliance**: BIR tax filing requirements met on time
- **Efficiency**: Automated workflows reduce manual effort
- **Transparency**: Clear ownership and accountability (RACI)
- **Quality**: Built-in validation and review gates

## Critical Deadlines

### Internal (Company)
- **Day 5**: Bank reconciliation complete
- **Day 7**: All journal entries posted
- **Day 10**: Financial statements ready for review
- **Day 12**: Month-end close complete

### External (BIR)
- **Day 10**: Form 1601-C (Withholding Tax) - filed and paid
- **Day 15**: Form 2550M (Monthly VAT) - optional filing
- **Day 25**: Form 2550Q (Quarterly VAT) - quarterly only

## Standard Close Cycle

```
Day 1-2:   Bank Reconciliation
Day 3-4:   AP/AR Reconciliation
Day 5-6:   Fixed Assets & Depreciation
Day 7-8:   Inventory & COGS Adjustments
Day 9-10:  Journal Entries & Accruals
Day 11-12: Financial Statement Preparation
Day 13-14: Review & Approval
Day 15:    Period Lock & Archive
```

## Roles

- **Finance Supervisor**: Day-to-day execution, reconciliations, JE preparation
- **Senior Finance Manager**: Review, approval, BIR filing oversight
- **Finance SSC Manager**: Strategic oversight, policy compliance, automation sponsor
- **Accounting Staff**: Data entry, document processing, reconciliation support
