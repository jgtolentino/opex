# Glossary

## A

**Approval Matrix**: A table defining who has authority to approve specific types of requests based on role and request type.

## B

**BPMN**: Business Process Model and Notation - a graphical representation for specifying business processes in a workflow.

**Business Case**: A justification for a proposed project or undertaking, typically including costs, benefits, and strategic alignment.

## E

**Escalation**: The process of elevating an issue to higher levels of management or authority when resolution cannot be achieved at the current level.

## H

**HRIS**: Human Resources Information System - software for managing employee data, payroll, benefits, and HR processes.

**Headcount**: The total number of employees or the number of positions budgeted for a department.

## R

**RACI Matrix**: A responsibility assignment matrix that defines Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed parties for each task.

**Requisition**: A formal request for goods, services, or personnel, typically requiring approval before fulfillment.

## S

**SLA**: Service Level Agreement - a commitment between a service provider and client defining expected service quality and response times.

**SOP**: Standard Operating Procedure - a documented set of step-by-step instructions for completing a routine task.
